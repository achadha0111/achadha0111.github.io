Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are tickets free?

A: Yes. They are 100% free!

Q: Who can get a ticket?

A: Enrolled students or students who graduated in the last 12 months.

Q: What if I study outside the UK?

A: You are still eligible. If you are outside Europe, you may need to apply for a visa. We can't sponsor visas.

Q: I haven't coded much. Should I come?

A: Hackathons are for beginners. Networking with and seeing awesome hackers in action will expose you to a tremendous amount of knowledge. Amazing suit of industry experts and mentors that are happy to assist you!

Q: What should I bring?

A: Bring your laptop, phone and chargers. Other things to consider: spare clothes, adapters, hardware and a sleeping bag, oh and don't forget your enthusiasm! We are going to provide you with all food, swag, sleeping rooms and drinks you will need to keep you going.

Q: I don't have a team. Help?

A: Don't let that discourage you - it is actually part of the fun! You will have 3 new friends by the end of the weekend. A team building session will be organized to help you form teams. We encourage everyone to use Devpost to find teammates.

Q: What can I expect from GreatUniHack?

A: An entire weekend of great company, good food and plenty of swag! We'll also have hardware and tons of mentors.

Q: I would like to mentor and give back to the community?

A: Awesome! Please fill in this form and we will get back to you with more details.

Q: Do you have a code of conduct?

A: Yes! We strictly adhere to the Major League Hacking Code of Conduct.

Q: Do you have safeguarding document?

A: Yes, we do! Please contact us for more information.

Q: Who owns my IP/code/idea?

A: You own all the rights to your intellectual property. We won't claim any right over anything made during this event.

Q: Who are the people behind this event?

A: We are HackSoc MCR, run by students from The University of Manchester and Manchester Metropolitan University We are extremely passionate about hackathons and tech events and we have run StudentHack and GreatUniHack successfully for 4 years in Manchester.

Q: What's the difference between StudentHack and GreatUniHack?

A: StudentHack and GreatUniHack both stemmed from MMU and Manchester University respectively. We're now working together to deliver an even more epic hackathon.

Q: I now love hackathons. How can I go to more?

A: There's a lot of hackathons! Check out the MLH Europe Spring 2016 schedule to see other fantastic hackathons.
