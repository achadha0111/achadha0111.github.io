The Partnership

Established in 2014, Entech Kunstoffe Solutions is a Joint Venture between the Gandhi Family, Promoters of T& Haresh G. Chandarana Family, Founders of Timo Plast, Monarch Plastics & Premauni Tools. Entech combines the extensive foresight and experience of the promoter groups, who have come together with a visionary thinking to provide customers with a complete and quality solutions in the Injection Molding Process. Our Core Strength lies in development & manufacture of customized solutions & systems for precision injection moldings of technical parts used in assemblies.

Management Team

Chandrana Family

Mr. Haresh Chandarana Founded Timo Plast in 1974 primarily for Injection Molding of small to medium sized precision parts. Established own tool room in name of Premauni Tools in 1982 that manufactured precision moulds for Injection Moulding.

Gandhi Family

Over 36 years of experience in the business of custom Injection Molding & Mold Making for medium to large sized parts of Engineering Thermoplastics for diverse range of industries such as automobiles, consumer electronics, Home Appliances and Textiles.

Our Vision

Commitment & Desire to give our customers maximum returns - with our expertise & deep understanding of technology as well as customer's specifications, dedication of our staff and our passion for technology.

Mission

  • To serve all sectors of engineering & product manufacturing with technologically sophisticated Molds, Systems & Injection Molded Parts.
  • The development and production of complex injection molds and their integration into processes.
  • Precision Machining of Parts for automobile, aerospace, electrical & medical industries.

Our Philosophy

  • Focus on people & technical innovation through sound corporate management achieved through:
  • Key focus on Training & Development
  • Planned Trainings for EDM, CNC Inspection, Best Practices, CAD / CAM Software Feature Update etc. every 2-3 months in co-ordination with OEM Technical Support Divisions.
  • 100% trainee acceptance rate & turnaround of less than 1%
  • Project Management Approach with CFT Participation within the organization keeping customer in focus
  • Flat Hierarchies & direct communication to ensure quick & well thought out decisions
  • Investment in the most contemporary technology, equipments & facilities from reputed brands

Value Proposition

  • Adoption of Latest Technology - 5 - Axis CNC Milling Machine, System 3R
  • Modular tool design based on market-available standard parts.
  • In house execution of all process related steps:
    1. Part Design
    2. Engineering Optimization (Material, Process & Cost feasibilities)
    3. Mold design
    4. Tool making & Production
    5. Assembly & final inspection.
  • Highest level of professionalism at every stage of project planning and punctuality

Our Quality Policy - Scientific Approach to Develop Systems & Procedures throughout the manufacturing life cycle that ensures quality will follow.

Our Goals

  2. In House Wire EDM, CMM & Visual Measuring System by March 2017
  3. ISO 9001 Certification by December 2016
  4. 2 Additional VMCs in Calendar Year 2016 & 2017
  5. In house Injection Molding Facility with assembly in FY 2016-17
  6. Expansion with Green field manufacturing facility by 2018
  7. To provide completely integrated and customized solutions, from first design studies to demanding pilot and clean room production by 2018.
